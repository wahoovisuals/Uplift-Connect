How to Apply for Welfare Schemes

Applying for government welfare schemes can be straightforward if the right documents and steps are followed. Here's a basic guide:

  • Identify the scheme that suits your need (education, housing, food security, etc.).
  • Check eligibility criteria on the official government portal.
  • Prepare necessary documents: ID proof, income certificate, address proof, etc.
  • Submit your application online or at a designated government office.
  • Track your application status and follow up if required.
