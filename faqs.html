FAQs About Poverty Alleviation

1. Who is eligible for welfare schemes?

Eligibility varies depending on the scheme but generally includes income thresholds, caste or community classification, and residency requirements.

2. What documents are required?

Commonly needed documents include ID proof, income certificate, Aadhaar card, and residential proof.

3. How long does it take for approval?

It depends on the scheme and location. Some applications are processed within weeks, others may take longer.
